# Privacy & Terms Color Fixes

## Overview

Fixed color visibility issues on the Privacy Policy and Terms & Conditions screens.

## Changes Made

### 1. Privacy Policy Screen (`privacy_policy_screen.dart`)

- **AppBar**: Changed background to `AppTheme.primaryColor` (Purple) and text to White.
- **Headings**: Changed text color from White to `AppTheme.textColor` (Dark).
- **Body Text**: Changed text color from White to `AppTheme.textColor` (Dark).

### 2. Terms & Conditions Screen (`terms_screen.dart`)

- **AppBar**: Changed background to `AppTheme.primaryColor` (Purple) and text to White.
- **Headings**: Changed text color from White to `AppTheme.textColor` (Dark).
- **Body Text**: Changed text color from White to `AppTheme.textColor` (Dark).

## Result

These screens are now fully readable on the white background, consistent with the new light theme.
